Animal Testing: Is it Necessary?

Animals are used in analyses by scientists probing for cures for a variety of dangerous diseases that can disrupt human life. They are also used by pharmaceutical and cosmetic companies to evaluate the safety of drugs, makeup, lotions, soaps, and shampoos. Why do we rely on animals to test our products for human safety? The reason is in the fact that animals share many genes and organs with humans; because of this, scientists say, using animals enhances knowledge while it likewise protects humans from presumably harmful effects of new products.

If animal testing is designed to protect humans, then why do so many people react negatively to it? Today, more and more people refused to purchase products tested on animals because they consider animal testing cruel, inhumane, and unnecessary.

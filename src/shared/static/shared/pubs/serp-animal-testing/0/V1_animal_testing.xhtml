Animal Testing: Is it Necessary?

Animals are used in studies by scientists. They use animals to look for cures for disease. Animals are also used by drug and makeup companies to test drugs, makeup, lotions, soaps, and shampoos. Why do we rely on animals to test for safety? Because animals share many genes and organs with humans. Scientists say that using animals boosts knowledge and protects humans from possibly harmful effects of new products.

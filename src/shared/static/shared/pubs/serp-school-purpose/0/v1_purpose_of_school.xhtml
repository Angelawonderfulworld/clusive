What is the Purpose of School?

Why do we go to school? Some people think the main goal of education is to give knowledge to students. They feel there is definite information that kids should know. For example, they want kids to know what happened in the Revolutionary War. They want them to know how the food chain works. Others interpret the role of school as one of getting kids ready to get a good job. They are most worried about students learning particular skills. They think reading, writing, and arithmetic are very important for kids to learn.

Some argue that schools should introduce a set of shared values, including liberty and justice. They believe this will help students understand the structure of our government. They feel it is important for students to understand that each branch of government has a different function. They also want them to know that the three branches work together. Government works to make sure we all enjoy certain freedoms and live by the same rules.

Some think schools should teach students to carefully analyze what they see, hear, and read. They want students to be able to think carefully about different perspectives. They want students to respect and challenge other viewpoints. They want them to form their own opinions about issues that affect them. Although many people say that they want kids to be able to think for themselves, students do not always have the freedom to do so in the classroom.
